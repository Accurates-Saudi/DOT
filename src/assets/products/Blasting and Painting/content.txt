Blasting and Painting
Overview
 DOT provides abrasive blasting and industrial painting services to prepare and protect equipment for corrosive oil and gas environments.
 Surface preparation and coating application are performed to support long-term durability and compliance with project coating specifications.
Applications
 Downhole and surface equipment finishing
 Structural and fabricated assemblies
 Refurbishment of used oilfield equipment
Features
 Abrasive blasting for surface profile preparation
 Industrial coating systems per specification
 Controlled application environment
Benefits
 Enhanced corrosion protection
 Improved equipment appearance and durability
 Specification-compliant coating delivery
