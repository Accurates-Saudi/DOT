Bead Peening
Overview
 DOT offers bead peening services to induce beneficial compressive residual stress in metal surfaces, improving fatigue resistance and durability of critical components.
 The process is applied to downhole tools, machined parts, and welded areas where extended service life is required in harsh operating environments.
Applications
 Downhole tools and completion equipment
 Welded joints and stress concentration areas
 Components subject to cyclic loading
Features
 Controlled peening intensity and coverage
 Suitable for a range of alloy materials
 Process documentation and traceability
Benefits
 Improved fatigue and stress corrosion resistance
 Extended component service life
 Reduced risk of surface-initiated failures
