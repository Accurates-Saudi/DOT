Pipe Threader and Phosphating
Overview
 DOT provides pipe threading and phosphating services for oilfield tubulars and industrial pipe products.
 Phosphating delivers a protective conversion coating that improves corrosion resistance and prepares surfaces for storage, transport, and subsequent coating operations.
Applications
 OCTG and casing connections
 Tubular products for oil and gas wells
 Industrial pipe preparation

 Process control for repeatable quality

 Improved corrosion protection
 Thread integrity and connection reliability
