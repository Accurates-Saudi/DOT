Pressure Test (Hydro and Gas)
Overview
 DOT performs hydrostatic and gas pressure testing to verify the integrity of pressure-containing equipment before field deployment.
 Testing is conducted using calibrated equipment with documented procedures to confirm compliance with client and industry requirements.
Applications
 Tubulars, valves, and pressure vessels
 Completion and well service equipment
 Fabricated assemblies prior to dispatch
Features
 Hydrostatic and gas pressure test capability
 Calibrated test equipment and gauges
 Test reports and certification documentation
Benefits
 Verified pressure integrity before installation
 Reduced risk of in-service failures
 Full traceability and documentation support
